ABOUT ME

I was born In 4/9/1994 in Abadan/Iran. Playing Piano and Tombak , I Prefer to Listen To Music Instead of listening to what people say , I Prefer to play music instead of talking to people.

STAFF STATEMENT



I think what had to be said was said already. But human being needs to talk all the time and I am not an exception. However I prefer my music to be my words. This album is a start of my long speech. A speech to be read not by my tongue but by my musical instruments.

7 SECONDS OF SILENCE

Before taking an espresso, drink a glass of water to get rid of whatever you had beforehand so you have a cut clear moment, once you play the album from the beginning you will have 7 second of silence. concentrate for 6 seconds to clear whatever noise your ears heard.

ARTWORK

The spaceship in the Artwork represents a music playing device. The long antenna on top of the spaceship represents a microphone. In the right hand side you can see 5 electricity wires that are representing the five-line staff and the birds that are sitting on or flying around the lines are representing the music notes. The main message that this artwork tries to deliver is the base of the spaceship that looks like a cage and inside the cage you can find the Rest which is an interval of silence in a piece of music that is in bondage.
